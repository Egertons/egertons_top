日本の地名

日本国（日语：にほんこく），简称“日本”，领土由北海道、本州、四国、九州四个大岛及6800多个小岛组成。

The state of Japan, referred to as "Japan", consists of four large islands, Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku and Kyushu, and more than 6800 small islands.
